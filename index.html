About SAMADHAN

SAMADHAN is a National Social Innovation Challenge bringing together students from IITs, IIMs, and leading institutions to co-create solutions for NGOs and grassroots communities.

This initiative bridges the gap between academic excellence and ground-level social challenges, empowering the next generation of changemakers to develop sustainable, scalable solutions that transform lives.

Our Vision

A future where every child has a dignified childhood and equal opportunity to live, learn, and grow.
